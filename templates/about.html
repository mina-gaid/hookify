About

What is Hookify?

Hookify is a Web application to discover music recommendations based on your favorite artists. Using advanced Machine Learning Algorithms to find recommendations based on artists, Hookify uses data to help you find songs that match your taste. Recommendations include both songs from the searched artist and others that may match your tast of music. Results are sorted using advanced, self learning, artificial intelligence before being displayed based on ranking.

Hookify is able to determine the type of music you like with the use of data from services such as Spotify and YouTube. Data that comes from similar search results used by others on those services.

Privacy

Hookify Does Not Collect or Store Data of it's Users or of Any Kind

The right to Privacy is important. The app is monetized using (Google) ads, not by mining your data. It is for that reason that Hookify uses HookTube instead of YouTube for playing songs. While the source might be from YouTube, HookTube is used to redirect so to allow for the following -

  • - Play videos without giving them views
  • - Bypasses country blocks & age restrictions
  • - Keep your data private from Google

Hookify has no need to learn your location or identity.
We simply don't care, and if we wanted to know more about you, we'll do it over Coffee, not an IP address.
